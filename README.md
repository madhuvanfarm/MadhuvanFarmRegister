# Madhuvan Farm - Sugarcane Business Management Platform

A premium, localized platform designed for managing sugarcane deliveries, attendance, and financial records for Madhuvan Farm.

## Features

- **Sugarcane Entry Register**: Tracks inward and outward sugarcane transactions (Deliveries, Bijane Apeli, Borrowing).
- **Staff Attendance & Payroll**: Manage labor and staff attendance with consolidated reporting.
- **Diesel Expense Tracker**: Real-time tracking of fuel consumption and costs per tractor.
- **Real-time Dashboard**: Comprehensive financial and operational summary.
- **Export Capabilities**: Generate professional CSV/Excel reports for all registers.
